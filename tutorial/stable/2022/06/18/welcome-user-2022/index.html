Causal Machine Learning with DoubleML

Welcome to the UseR!2022 tutorial “Causal machine learning with DoubleML” taking place virtually on June 20, 2022 from 4:00 to 7:30am CDT.

On this website you’ll find all materials that are used during the tutorial. We will share the materials with participants here just before the tutorial starts.

The tutorial is split into four parts:

  1. Introduction to Double Machine Learning
    • Slides, Speaker notes
    • Shiny app Key Ingredients of Double Machine Learning
  2. Introduction to the DoubleML Package for R
    • Slides, Speaker notes
    • Demand Elasticity Notebook (.Rmd, .ipynb)
  3. Hands-on Tutorial
    • AB Testing Notebook (.Rmd, .ipynb)
  4. Summary, Discussion, Feedback
    • Slides
